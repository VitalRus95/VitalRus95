## Vital

### ENGLISH
Hi, my name is Vitaly Pavlovich Ulyanov, also known by the nickname **Vital**. I am a [GTA scripter](https://github.com/VitalRus95/gta-scripts) (GTA 3D series, _singleplayer only_) and author of many [scripting video tutorials](https://www.youtube.com/c/VitalRUS13/featured) (available in Russian). Apart from game modding, I sometimes make my own simple programmes in [HTML+CSS+JS](https://github.com/VitalRus95/HTML-Programmes).
